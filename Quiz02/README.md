# Quiz 02 — Constraint Satisfaction Problems & Regression

## Course

Artificial Intelligence (Spring 2025)

---

## Overview

This quiz was collaboratively designed during my Teaching Assistantship for the undergraduate Artificial Intelligence course.

I personally contributed to the design of several analytical and conceptual questions, with particular emphasis on Constraint Satisfaction Problems (CSP), consistency algorithms, optimization, and regression.

The quiz was designed to evaluate students' understanding of both theoretical concepts and mathematical reasoning rather than memorization.

---

## Topics

- Constraint Satisfaction Problems (CSP)

- K-Consistency
- Path Consistency
- Dual Graph Transformation

- LCV
- Gradient Descent
- Batch Gradient Descent
- Linear Regression
- Optimization
- Contour Analysis

---

## Learning Objectives

Students are expected to

- formulate CSPs mathematically
- execute AC-3 step-by-step
- analyze higher-order consistency
- understand constraint propagation
- compare optimization algorithms
- analyze gradient descent behavior
- reason about convergence properties
- manually update regression parameters
