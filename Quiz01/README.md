# Quiz 01 — Search Algorithms

## Course

Artificial Intelligence (Spring 2025)

---

## Overview

This quiz was designed for undergraduate Artificial Intelligence students and focuses on classical search algorithms.

The questions emphasize algorithmic reasoning rather than memorization and require students to analyze search procedures, complexity, and heuristic behavior.

Several questions in this quiz were originally designed by me during my Teaching Assistantship.

---

## Topics

- Depth-First Search (Tree Search)
- Depth-First Search (Graph Search)
- Breadth-First Search
- A*
- Goal Test at Generation Time
- Goal Test at Expansion Time
- Search Trees
- Complexity Analysis

---

## Learning Objectives

Students are expected to

- distinguish Tree Search from Graph Search
- analyze traversal order
- construct A* search trees
- reason about admissible search behavior
- analyze branching factor constraints
- derive BFS expansion formulas
